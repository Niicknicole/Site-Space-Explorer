Types of Black Holes

  • Stellar: Born from collapsed stars.
  • Supermassive: Found at the center of galaxies, millions of times the Sun's mass.
  • Intermediate: Rare, forming a bridge between stellar and supermassive.
  • Primordial: Hypothetical tiny black holes formed in the early universe.

How It Works
